What is FSAE?

The Formula SAE series Competitions challenge teams of university undergraduate students to conceive,design,fabricate,develop and compete,across the globe with formula style prototype race car.

The teams are tested in various events which include dynamic and static properties of their car,testing the quality of their manufacturing process,driver's ability and pitching their car to venture capitalists via Business presentation.
